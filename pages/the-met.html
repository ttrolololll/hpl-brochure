The Met Condominium
Bangkok

A multiple award-winning residential skyscraper in Bangkok, The Met is designed to offer cool and green living in dense, urban conditions. Composed of six towers connected by sky gardens, a clever combination of sunshades, overhangs and walls of live greenery filter sunlight and protect interiors from overheating, while apertures enhance passing breezes to cool the various residential units. Besides winning the 2009 Bronze Emporis Skyscraper Award, The Met was also named Design of the Year 2009 at the President’s Design Awards in Singapore. In 2010, it won the prestigious International Highrise Award in Germany and was lauded for its efforts to enhance sustainable living conditions in the tropical region.
