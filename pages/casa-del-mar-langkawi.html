Casa Del Mar
Langkawi

With a perfectly poetic name that translates into "Home by the sea" in Spanish, Casa del Mar is a serene paradise nestled on the pristine Pantai Cenang beach of Langkawi island, a UNESCO World Geopark site in Peninsula Malaysia. Designed to embrace the tranquility of seaside living, within walking distance of the island's best local cafes and restaurants, it is ideal for travellers looking for a private and peaceful oasis.
