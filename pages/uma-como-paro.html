Uma by COMO,
Paro, Bhutan

A holistically intimate 29-room resort ensconced in the Paro Valley, Uma by COMO, Paro immerses you in Bhutan's richly textured culture while reveling in the socially conscious and luxurious sensibilities of a COMO resort. Combining local artisanship with COMO's contemporary style, the resort's enviable location lets you take in soul-stirring views of forest, mountains and the surrounding valley in absolute privacy; at the same time offering you easy access to the many cultural landmarks and highlights of this Buddhist country.
