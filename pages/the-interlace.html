The Interlace

One of the largest and most ambitious residential developments in Singapore, The Interlace presents a radically new approach to fuse urban living with nature. Designed by Ole Scheeren, partner of OMA, it adopts a new residential typology to integrate communal spaces into the surrounding lush expanses. The result is a breathtaking hexagonal formation of stacked apartment blocks, arranged to maximise the surrounding flora with extensive roof gardens, sky terraces, cascading balconies and lush green areas to create a uniquely continuous landscape.
